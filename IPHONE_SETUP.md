# 📱 How to Add TFT Team Builder to iPhone Home Screen

## Super Simple (30 seconds)

### Step 1: Open in Safari
1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Go to wherever you uploaded the `index.html` file
   - **Local**: Open the file directly
   - **GitHub**: `yourusername.github.io/tft-team-builder/`
   - **Vercel**: `your-app.vercel.app`
   - **Netlify**: `your-app.netlify.app`

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen
1. Tap the **Share button** (↑) at the bottom
2. Scroll and find **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Keep or change the name (I recommend "TFT Builder")
4. Tap **"Add"**

### Done! ✅
- TFT logo appears on home screen
- Tap it to open the app
- Works offline!
- Full screen (no Safari UI)

## Troubleshooting

**"Add to Home Screen option not showing?"**
- Make sure you're in Safari (not Chrome)
- Tap Share button at bottom center
- Scroll right to see more options

**"App won't open?"**
- Refresh Safari
- Close and reopen the app
- Delete and re-add to home screen

**"Teams not saving?"**
- Make sure Safari allows cookies/storage
- Not in private/incognito mode
